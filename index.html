What is a Private Key?

Your private key is a unique cryptographic code that grants access to your wallet. It usually looks like:

0x4c0883a6910395b1e8f2...
  • Starts with 0x followed by 64 hexadecimal characters
  • Found in your wallet app under "Export Private Key" or "Security"
  • Never share this with anyone — it controls your funds
Encrypted & secured locally on your device
